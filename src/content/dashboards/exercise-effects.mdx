**The Dashboard of the Study outlined metabolic, inflammatory and trace elements.**  
Metabolic (Glucose, Lactate), Inflammatory (WBC, GRA, LYM, NLR, SII, SIRI), Trace Elements (Selenium, SELENOP, Zinc, Free Zinc, Copper, Iron).  
Each can be switched on/off independently; at least one always stays active.  
**Adjustment toggle:** Unadjusted vs. hemoconcentration-adjusted. Parameters without adjusted values (NLR, Free Zinc) are automatically grayed out in adjusted mode.  
**Display toggle:** % Change from pre-exercise baseline (default, all parameters on a common scale) or Absolute values.  
**Exercise zone:** amber shaded area from −1 min to 0 min marking the 1-minute STST, with a bold dashed line at 0 min and a "STST" label.  
**±1 SD bands** — drawn as a transparent fill around each line. Each parameter in the legend is also clickable to individually show/hide lines.
